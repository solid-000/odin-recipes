Cranberry Sauce


Description

Skip the store-bought stuff this year and serve homemade cranberry sauce. It's actually way easier than you think. You can make this top-rated recipe, which has more than 2,000 rave reviews from Allrecipes community members, with just three ingredients. Learn how to make, store, freeze, and serve the best cranberry sauce ever.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Cranberries
  • Sugar
  • Orange juice

Steps

  1. Heat orange juice in a saucepan.
  2. Stir in sugar and cook until it dissolves.
  3. Add cranberries and continue cooking, stirring often, until they start to pop.
  4. Remove the sauce from heat and transfer it to a bowl, the cranberry sauce will continue to thicken as it cools.
